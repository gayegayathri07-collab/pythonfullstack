Welcome to MTIET

Mother Theresa Institutions Engineering College (MTIET) is a premier engineering institution dedicated to nurturing technically competent, ethically strong, and socially responsible engineers. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, industry-aligned curriculum, and experienced faculty, we provide an unparalleled learning environment.

Our campus is spread across a lush green landscape with modern laboratories, smart classrooms, a vast library, and world-class sports facilities. We focus on holistic development through academics, research, innovation, and extracurricular activities.

  • NAAC 'A' Grade Accredited
  • UGC Autonomous Status
  • Awarded Two Gold Medals by JNTUH
  • 100% Admissions Record
  • Industry-Recognized & Globally Connected
